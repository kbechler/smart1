ESP module: ESP-12E (ESP8266)
POWER:      Mini USB connector ("Mini USB SMD 5 Pin Female Mini B Socket Connector Plug")
MOSFETs:    3x AO3400
OUTPUTs:    - Pin header (GND-SIGNAL-VDD) for WS2816
            - Pin header (VDD-CH1-CH2-CH3) for RGB LEDs
Case:       70x45x18mm ("DIY 70x45x18mm Case")

GPIO0  - SWITCH #1
GPIO4  - WS2818 output
GPIO5  - SWITCH #2
GPIO12 - RGB LED channel #1
GPIO13 - RGB LED channel #2
GPIO14 - RGB LED channel #3
